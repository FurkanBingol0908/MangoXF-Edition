Welcome to Our Micro:Bit House Projects

Welcome to our Grade 10 final project website! We created two amazing things for our cardboard house using Micro:Bit. These projects make our house more like a real home by adding working parts that you can control.

Our cardboard house has two special features that make it unique:

  • A working fan that moves air through the house
  • A garage door that opens and closes automatically

1. Micro:Bit Fan by Furkan

This fan helps keep our cardboard house comfortable by moving air through all the rooms. Furkan created it using a Micro:Bit and a motor. You can control it with simple button presses:

  • Press button A to start the fan spinning
  • Press button B to stop it
  • The fan moves air through every room in the house
See how the fan works

2. Garage Door Opener by Xavier

Xavier built this automatic garage door that works just like a real one. It's perfect for our cardboard house because:

  • The door moves up and down smoothly
  • You can control it with Micro:Bit buttons
  • It makes our house more realistic
Learn about the garage door

Why We Made These Projects

We wanted to show how simple technology can make things more interesting. Our cardboard house isn't just a model - it has working parts that make it special. These projects helped us learn about:

  • How to use Micro:Bit to control things
  • Making our ideas work in real life
  • Creating useful features for a model house
