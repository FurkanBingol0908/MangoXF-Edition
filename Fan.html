Micro:Bit Fan Project

Made by Furkan

What is this project?

This is a small fan that I made for our cardboard house project. The fan uses a Micro:Bit and a servo motor to create moving air inside the house, just like a real house fan. When you press buttons on the Micro:Bit, the fan starts spinning and moves air through all the rooms in our cardboard house.

How does it work?

The fan is really simple to use. Here's what it does:

  • When you press the A button on the Micro:Bit, the fan starts spinning
  • Press the B button, and the fan stops
  • The fan spins around smoothly to move air through the house
  • The fan is placed in the main room of our house to spread air everywhere

Why is it useful for our cardboard house?

This fan makes our cardboard house project much more interesting and realistic. Here's why it's important:

  • It shows how real houses need air movement to stay comfortable
  • The fan demonstrates how we can use simple machines in our project
  • It makes our cardboard house feel more like a real home
  • When you look inside the house, you can see the fan working just like a real one
  • It's a great example of how we can use Micro:Bit to control things in our house
