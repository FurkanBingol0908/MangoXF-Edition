Micro:Bit Garage Door Project

Made by Xavier

What is this project?

I made this automatic garage door for our cardboard house project. It's a working door that moves up and down smoothly, just like a real garage door. The door uses a Micro:Bit to control when it opens and closes, making our cardboard house more realistic and fun to use.

How does it work?

The garage door is simple to use. Here's what it does:

  • Press the A button on the Micro:Bit to make the door go up
  • Press the B button to make the door come down
  • The door moves smoothly on its track
  • It stops by itself when it's fully open or closed
  • You can open and close it as many times as you want

Why is it useful for our cardboard house?

This garage door makes our cardboard house much more interesting. Here's why it's important:

  • It works just like a real garage door, making our house more realistic
  • You can put toy cars through the door, just like a real garage
  • Visitors can try opening and closing it themselves
  • It shows how we can use Micro:Bit to make things move in useful ways
  • The door makes our house look more complete and finished
